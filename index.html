about

hello! i'm halida astatin, and i'm originally from bandung, indonesia. i finished my undergraduate study in informatics engineering a while ago. i flew over 10,000 miles away from home to start my masters in educational technology & applied learning sciences (metals!) at carnegie mellon university.

i joined metals because i love human-computer interaction and i want to contribute to education. there are remote areas that are lacking educators, and i believe educational technology should be the solution to this.

i'm somewhat experienced in web development, especially front-end. i've joined several different startups in bandung as well as taken a number of freelance web development projects. browsing web design showcase is one of my hobbies.

other things i enjoy immensely includes chocolates, photography, and tv series.
