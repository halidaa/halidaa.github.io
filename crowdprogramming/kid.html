Instruction

You will be playing a game as a kid avoiding monsters. Move your character left and right using the arrow keyboards to avoid incoming monsters. For every second you stay alive, you will get $0.001 bonus. The game ends when you either collect a total of $2.00 bonus, or get caught by a monster. If you play as a monster, the game ends when you collect a total of $1.50 bonus. You can also opt to leave the game any time by clicking the finish button.
