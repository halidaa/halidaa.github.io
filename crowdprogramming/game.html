Instruction

You will be playing a game as a monster chasing kids. Your goal is to catch as many kids as possible. You can only catch a kid coming from the top (you can't catch a kid from the right/left). For every kid you catch, you will get $0.001 bonus. You can only move sideways in the game. Use the right and left arrows on your keyboard to move your character. The game ends when you collect a total of $1.00 bonus. You can also leave the game any time by clicking the quit button.
